The Mission

We’re a coalition of undergraduates writing about science. At our respective universities, we publish, online or in print, articles about the cutting edge in science research today.

But that’s not enough. We can do more when we join together, and we’re going to launch an intercollegiate science journal that features the best of science writing and science communication.
